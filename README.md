# Boogie Woogie

A Caves of Qud mod that adds the Boogie Woogie mental mutation from Jujutsu Kaisen!

## Features

### Boogie Woogie Mental Mutation
- Clap your hands to instantly swap positions with any target in range
- Can target creatures, objects, or even inanimate items
- Multi-object selection when right-clicking tiles with multiple valid targets
- Confuses targets on swap (Willpower save to resist)
- Scales with mutation level: increased range, shorter cooldown, higher confusion DC

### Vibroslap Cudgels
Unique cudgel weapons that grant Boogie Woogie when equipped:

| Material | Tier | Damage | Penetration | Free Action | BW Level |
|----------|------|--------|-------------|-------------|----------|
| Wooden | 1 | 1d4 | 1 | 15% | 1 |
| Bronze | 2 | 1d4+1 | 2 | 20% | 2 |
| Iron | 3 | 1d4+2 | 3 | 25% | 3 |
| Steel | 4 | 1d6 | 4 | 30% | 4 |
| Carbide | 5 | 1d6+2 | 5 | 35% | 5 |
| Fullerite | 6 | 1d8 | 6 | 40% | 6 |
| Folded Carbide | 7 | 1d8+2 | 7 | 45% | 7 |
| Zetachrome | 8 | 1d10 | 8 | 50% | 8 |

- Spawns naturally in the world alongside other cudgels
- Unlocks the Boogie Woogie Repeat ability

### Boogie Woogie Repeat
- Requires vibroslap equipped
- Mark multiple targets and shuffle their positions 3-5 times rapidly
- Each shuffle happens with a satisfying *CLAP* message
- Spectacular mass confusion effect

### Vibrating Palm Cybernetics
Cybernetic hand implants available in all material tiers:

- Replaces one hand with a vibrating palm weapon
- Grants permanent Boogie Woogie mutation
- Higher tiers provide:
  - Stronger mutation levels (1-8)
  - Better free action chance (25-60%)
  - Higher confusion save DC bonuses (+1 to +4)
  - More powerful hand attacks
- Installs on Hand body slots (1-4 license points depending on tier)

## Inspired by Jujutsu Kaisen

Boogie Woogie is Todo Aoi's cursed technique from the anime/manga Jujutsu Kaisen. By clapping his hands, he can swap the positions of anything with cursed energy within his range. This mod brings that mechanic to Caves of Qud!

- Cybernetics mods (especially direct install mods)
- Weapon collection mods

## Usage

### As a Mental Mutation
- Clap to target and swap with anything in range
- Right-click to select specific objects on crowded tiles
- Higher levels = longer range, shorter cooldown

### With Vibroslaps
- Equip any vibroslap cudgel to gain Boogie Woogie temporarily
- Use Boogie Woogie Repeat to mark and shuffle multiple targets
- Higher tier vibroslaps grant stronger mutation levels

### With Cybernetics
- Install vibrating palm implant on a hand slot
- Permanently gain Boogie Woogie at the implant's tier level
- Your hand becomes a spatial-warping cudgel weapon
